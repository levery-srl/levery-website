## About the project

Traditional building maintenance is largely reactive, relying on manual processes and paper-based records. This often leads to delayed interventions, increased downtime, and higher maintenance costs. PROMETHEUS delivered a groundbreaking XR-based WebXR application tailored for remote maintenance of adaptive building envelopes — enabling real-time, interactive maintenance via PCs and tablets, fostering seamless remote collaboration, and enhancing building maintenance efficiency.

The project is developed in collaboration with EXVIRIENCE S.r.l. and has been the foundation for the ARyze platform.

## Funding & context

PROMETHEUS is funded by the European Union. Views and opinions expressed are those of the authors only and do not necessarily reflect those of the European Union or EISMA. The project directly led to the commercialisation of the ARyze XR maintenance platform.

## Levery's role

- Definition of XR use cases for building operations and maintenance, including reporting workflows, documentation access and remote assistance scenarios.
- Integration of BIM, Digital Product Passport and building management data into the XR interface.
- Building O&M workflow design — translating maintenance processes into step-by-step AR-guided procedures.
- Technology transfer and commercialisation pathway from PROMETHEUS research outputs to the ARyze product.
