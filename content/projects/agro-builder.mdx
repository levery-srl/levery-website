## About the project

Agro.build.ER develops a circular agro-waste value chain for the sustainable building sector, creating rural-urban synergies across Emilia-Romagna. The project identifies agricultural residues with construction material potential and builds the supply chain infrastructure — from collection and processing to product development and market placement — within the regional innovation ecosystem.

## Funding & context

Agro.build.ER is funded by the Regione Emilia-Romagna as part of regional circular economy and green innovation programmes. The project connects the region's strong agri-food sector with construction innovation.

## Levery's role

- Circular business model design — identifying viable pathways for agro-waste valorisation within the construction supply chain.
- Material valorisation strategy — technical assessment of agro-waste streams suitable for building product applications.
- Stakeholder engagement and supply chain mapping across producers, processors and construction companies in the region.
