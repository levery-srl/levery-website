## About the project

BELM (Built Environment Lifecycle Management Assistance) develops digital tools for whole-life assessment and management of building systems and components. The project addresses the lack of integrated digital infrastructure for tracking building performance, maintenance histories and environmental impacts across the full lifecycle — from construction to end-of-life.

## Funding & context

BELM is funded under Horizon Europe. The project builds on advances in digital twin technology, IoT sensor integration and lifecycle assessment methodology to create practical tools for building owners, facility managers and manufacturers.

## Levery's role

- Platform architecture — contributing to the design of the BELM digital infrastructure for lifecycle data management.
- Sustainability assessment modules — developing LCA and LCC calculation tools integrated within the platform.
- Data interoperability — ensuring BELM outputs are compatible with Digital Product Passport standards and ESPR reporting requirements.
