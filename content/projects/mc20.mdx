## About the project

The MC2.0 project demonstrates how automated manufacturing of Integrated Photovoltaic (IPV) elements using a mass customisation approach can deliver a cost breakthrough in IPV façades, windows and roofs. The overarching ambition is to make BIPV economically viable for mainstream construction by combining advanced manufacturing with flexible design.

## Funding & context

MC2.0 is funded by the European Union under Horizon Europe (Grant Agreement 101096139). The project consortium includes BIPV manufacturers, façade system integrators, research institutions and technology providers across Europe.

## Levery's role

- Market needs analysis for IPV products — conducting assessments to define testing protocols and ensure successful product deployment in the construction sector.
- Ecodesign MCDA methodology — developing an AI-augmented multi-criteria decision analysis framework for ESPR-aligned ecodesign prioritisation for BIPV manufacturers.
- LCA and LCC analysis for building use cases — evaluating lifecycle cost impact across specific applications to optimise production efficiency.
- Digital Product Passport design for BIPV products, aligned with ESPR regulatory requirements.
- Circularity assessment — implementing use cases that address circular economy principles for the next generation of IPV products.
