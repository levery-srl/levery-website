## About the project

BIOS MATER re-designs the construction sector through the development of sustainable and functional bio-based construction products with competitive market performance. The project addresses the full innovation chain — from raw material sourcing to product testing, certification and market deployment — for a new generation of construction materials derived from biological feedstocks.

## Funding & context

BIOS MATER is funded under Horizon Europe. The project targets construction product manufacturers looking to transition toward bio-based material portfolios ahead of upcoming ESPR regulatory requirements.

## Levery's role

- Product development support — technical guidance across material selection, formulation and prototype testing phases.
- Lifecycle assessment (LCA) to quantify environmental performance of bio-based products versus conventional alternatives.
- Market analysis — identifying target segments, regulatory pathways and competitive positioning for bio-based construction products.
- Pre-commercial validation — supporting manufacturers in preparing products for market entry, including documentation and certification strategy.
