## About the project

The BIO.CO.R.N.ER project proposes a new circular, regional value chain that transforms bio-waste from agri-food, aquaculture, and marine residues into eco-sustainable building materials. Based in the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy, the project builds on synergies between the agri-food industry and construction to pioneer low-carbon, bio-based mortars and building components.

## Funding & context

This project has received funding from the CIRCULOOS Horizon Europe project — 2nd Open Call (Grant Agreement 101092295). CIRCULOOS supports circular economy initiatives across European regions by connecting waste producers with material innovators in the construction sector.

## Levery's role

- Integration of CIRCULOOS digital tools, ensuring interoperability between existing systems and enabling efficient circular economy data flows across the supply chain.
- Research and development of digital traceability tools for the circular value chain, including NGSI-LD data modelling and Digital Product Passport design.
- Mapping of the information chain and identification of key actors across the bio-waste valorisation network in Emilia-Romagna.
