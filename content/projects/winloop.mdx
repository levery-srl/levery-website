## About the project

WINLOOP addresses one of the least circular product categories in construction — windows. The project develops technologies, digitalisation tools and business models to close the lifecycle of windows, from design for disassembly to end-of-life material recovery. WINLOOP targets both new windows and the existing stock of installed frames across European buildings.

## Funding & context

WINLOOP is funded under Horizon Europe. The project consortium brings together window manufacturers, recyclers, digital tool developers and research institutions across Europe.

## Levery's role

- Digital Product Passport design for window systems — structuring product data for circularity across the full lifecycle.
- Lifecycle assessment (LCA) of current and circular window scenarios, quantifying environmental benefits of circular business models.
- Circular business model definition — identifying viable end-of-life recovery and remanufacturing pathways for window manufacturers.
