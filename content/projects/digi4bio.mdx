## About the project

Digi4BioMat develops Digital Product Passport (DPP) infrastructure specifically for bio-based construction materials, enabling manufacturers to scale circular business models across EU supply chains. The project aligns DPP data structures with ESPR regulatory requirements and EPD reporting standards, creating interoperable digital tools that work across the construction value chain.

## Funding & context

Digi4BioMat is funded under Horizon Europe. The project directly informs the development of the DeePPy platform — Levery's commercial DPP tool for construction product manufacturers.

## Levery's role

- DPP platform design — defining data models, user interfaces and API structures for bio-based material passports.
- Regulatory alignment — mapping DPP data requirements against ESPR, EPD and circular economy reporting frameworks.
- Supply chain data architecture — designing systems for multi-tier supplier data collection and aggregation.
- Piloting DPP implementation with bio-based material manufacturers across the project consortium.
